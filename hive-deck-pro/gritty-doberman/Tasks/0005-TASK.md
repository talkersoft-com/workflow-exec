## Goal
Add `branch: hive` at the top of every deck YAML that targets the hive branch. Remove `default_target_branch` and `target_branches` from `config.yaml`.

## Repo
`workflow-configuration` at `/Users/talker/workspace/hive-deck-pro/workflow-configuration`

## Steps

```yaml

    vorch-cli: hive
    kvm-hosts: hive
```

And the comment above `target_branches`.

For each of the following deck files, add `branch: hive` as the very first non-comment line:

```bash
python3 -c "import yaml; [yaml.safe_load(open(f)) for f in __import__('glob').glob('decks/*.yaml')]"
```
